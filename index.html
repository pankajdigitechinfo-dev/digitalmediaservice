FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
Most business websites are delivered within 3–5 working days. E-commerce stores typically take 7–10 days. News portals are usually ready in 2–4 days. We always confirm a deadline before starting your project.
Do you provide hosting & domain?
Yes, we can help you purchase and set up hosting and domain registration. Hosting starts at approximately ₹1,500/year. We recommend trusted providers like Hostinger, Namecheap and GoDaddy for reliable performance in India.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept payments via UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), bank transfer, and all major debit/credit cards. Payment is split: 50% advance before starting work, 50% upon delivery and your satisfaction.
Do you provide after-sales support?
Absolutely! All packages include free support — 1 month for News Portal, 3 months for Business Website, and 6 months for E-Commerce. We are also available on WhatsApp for quick queries outside support periods.
Can you redesign my existing website?
Yes! We offer website redesign services. Share your current website URL with us on WhatsApp and we will provide a free audit and redesign quote within 24 hours.
Will my website rank on Google?
Every website we build is SEO-optimised with proper meta tags, schema markup, fast loading speeds and mobile responsiveness — all key Google ranking factors. For ongoing SEO campaigns, we offer dedicated monthly packages.
